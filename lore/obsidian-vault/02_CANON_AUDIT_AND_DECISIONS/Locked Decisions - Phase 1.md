# ✅ Locked Decisions — Phase 1

## Core naming
1. Aereth = world / server / setting.
2. RE:FRAGMENT = name for the event/condition that happened to Aereth.
3. Correct phrasing: **You are playing in Aereth after RE:FRAGMENT took place.**
4. The Erasure / Unmaking / Veiling are cultural or scholarly names for RE:FRAGMENT.
5. More regional names and theories can be added later.

## Gods
6. Aereth has no confirmed true gods.
7. “God” is a cultural label. Some peoples may worship forces, beings, remnants, constructs, or myths as gods.

## Identity and race
8. Remnant = condition shared by all players.
9. Former Remnant race is now **Continuants**.
10. Delvers are a launch playable race.
11. Delving may later be a profession/activity.
12. Delver factions may exist inside Delver society.
13. Archivists are a faction/profession order, not a race.

## Systems
14. Fragments do not grant raw power directly.
15. Fragments alter perception, responsibility, access, consequence, pressure, and world interaction.
16. Intent remains the meaning/direction system.
17. Conversion, Amplification, Redirection, Delay, Suppression, Distortion are now **Outcome Effects**.
18. Race, Profession, Discipline, Title, Fragment, Intent, and Outcome Effect are separate canon layers.

## Factions
19. Old Factions page is canon-source material and must be converted into current Aereth terms.
20. Factions are responses to loss, not nations.

## World map
21. Current six-continent map is not the full world.
22. The old map becomes the First Known Map / known-world prototype.
23. Aereth will be expanded greatly.
24. Fractured Expanse, Void Seas, and Absolute Void remain conceptually in the same places but become much larger.
25. World divisions: West, North, East, Center, South.
26. Center-West is the starting location.
27. Player progression generally moves West -> North -> East -> Center -> South.
28. New fog-covered unlockable continents can exist across divisions.
29. South is mainly endgame.

## Starter
30. Starter subregion is **The Lantern Marches**.
31. Main starter hub is **Lantern's Rest**.
