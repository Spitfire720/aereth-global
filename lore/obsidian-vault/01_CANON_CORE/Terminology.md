# 📘 Terminology

## Aereth
The world, server, and setting.

## RE:FRAGMENT
The world-changing event/condition that took place in Aereth. Player-facing phrasing: **You are playing in Aereth after RE:FRAGMENT took place.**

## The Erasure
Scholarly/system term for the continuity-damaging phenomenon associated with RE:FRAGMENT.

## The Unmaking
Historical or old-world term.

## The Veiling
Religious, folk, or mystic term.

## Remnant
A being who persists despite not being fully secure in reality after RE:FRAGMENT. All player characters are Remnants.

## Continuant
Launch playable race/origin. Human-descended survivors whose continuity remained mostly stable.

## Fragment
A persistent misalignment in reality attached to a player. Not loot. Not a raw power crystal.

## Intent
The meaning or direction the player imposes onto reality.

## Outcome Effect
The way an Intent-shaped action changes its result: Conversion, Amplification, Redirection, Delay, Suppression, or Distortion.

## Title
A world-recognition state. What Aereth remembers the player as.

## Discipline
A combat/metaphysical identity commitment unlocked later through progression.

## Profession
A societal, economic, crafting, exploration, or utility identity earned through play.
