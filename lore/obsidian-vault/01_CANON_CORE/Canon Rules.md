# 🔒 Canon Rules

> [!locked] Main principle
> Aereth is a world where continuity was damaged. Every lore element must connect to memory, proof, identity, loss, preservation, distortion, survival, or what returned wrong after RE:FRAGMENT.

## Absolute rules

1. Aereth is the world, server, and setting.
2. RE:FRAGMENT is the world-changing event/condition that happened to Aereth.
3. Correct phrasing: **You are playing in Aereth after RE:FRAGMENT took place.**
4. The Erasure, The Unmaking, and The Veiling are cultural/scholarly names for RE:FRAGMENT or aspects of it.
5. Aereth has no confirmed true gods.
6. “God” is a cultural label. Some groups may worship entities, remnants, constructs, forces, or myths as gods.
7. Remnant is a condition shared by all player characters.
8. Continuants are the former “Remnant race”: human-descended survivors whose continuity stayed mostly stable.
9. Delvers are a launch playable race.
10. Archivists are a faction/profession order, not a race.
11. Fragments do not grant raw power directly.
12. Intent is meaning/direction imposed by the player.
13. Outcome Effects describe how an action result changes.
14. Factions are responses to loss, not generic nations.
15. The current old map is the First Known Map, not the complete world.

> [!mystery]
> Mystery is allowed. Random confusion is not.
