# 🏷️ Canon Status Categories

- **Locked Canon**: accepted source of truth.
- **Canon with Terminology Update**: accepted idea, old wording must be converted.
- **Provisional Canon**: usable, but needs later review.
- **Draft**: not canon yet.
- **Deprecated**: removed or replaced.
- **Contradiction**: known conflict requiring decision.
- **Source Import**: raw or summarized old source material.
