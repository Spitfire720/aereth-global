# 💠 Fragments

> [!locked] Fragment law
> Fragments are not items, loot, upgrades, collectibles, or simple unlock flags.

Fragments are persistent misalignments in reality that attach to players. They change perception before mechanics and always increase responsibility.

## Non-negotiable laws
1. Fragments never grant raw power directly.
2. Fragments always increase responsibility.
3. Fragments change perception before mechanics.
4. Fragments are never neutral.
5. Fragments cannot be freely removed.
6. Fragments interact differently with each profession.

## Canon families
- [[Fracture Fragments]] — break the world.
- [[Anchor Fragments]] — resist the world.
- [[Distortion Fragments]] — lie to the world.
- [[Memory Fragments]] — remember the world.
