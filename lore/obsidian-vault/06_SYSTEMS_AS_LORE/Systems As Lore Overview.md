# ⚙️ Systems As Lore Overview

Aereth systems are not generic RPG mechanics wearing lore hats. They express the world condition after [[RE-FRAGMENT]].

| Layer | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Race | what the world reads your body/origin as |
| Profession | what you do in society/economy/crafting/exploration |
| Fragment | what broken reality attached to you |
| Intent | what meaning/direction you impose |
| Outcome Effect | how that meaning changes results |
| Discipline | what combat/metaphysical identity you become |
| Title | what Aereth remembers you as |
