# 📊 Stats Foundation

Race affects stats. Stats affect combat. Fragments affect pressure and abilities. Intent affects modifiers and playstyle. Discipline defines ability identity. Mutation transforms Discipline identity.

## Primary stats
vitality, strength, dexterity, intelligence, willpower, endurance.

## Derived stats
max health, attack power, defense, magic power, resistance, stability, erasure pressure, erasure resistance, movement speed, evasion, crit chance, crit damage.
