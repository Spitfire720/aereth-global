# 📈 Progression Overview

Level cap: 60.

```text
XP to next = floor(900 * level^1.9)
```

| Level | Phase | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1-15 | Discovery | The player is undefined and learning what they are. |
| 16-35 | Commitment | The player commits to a Discipline. |
| 36-50 | Optimization | The player sharpens identity. |
| 51-60 | Mastery / Mutation | Identity transforms under pressure. |

No starting classes. Race comes first. Discipline comes later.
