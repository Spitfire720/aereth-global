# 🌫️ Fogbound Regions

Fogbound regions are canon. The world is incomplete by design.

## Fog types

### Cartographic Fog
Unmapped, unexplored future expansion land.

### Erasure Fog
Places that refuse stable mapping. Labels fade, routes change, borders move, locations exist inconsistently.

### Political Fog
Known places deliberately hidden by Archivists, Continuum cells, Vestige Guilds, Cartotheca restrictions, or other powers.

### Mythic Fog
Places known only through tribal names, old myths, religious accounts, and contradictory maps.

### Void Fog
Territory near the Void Seas or Absolute Void. Unknown not because it is unexplored, but because continuity fails there.
