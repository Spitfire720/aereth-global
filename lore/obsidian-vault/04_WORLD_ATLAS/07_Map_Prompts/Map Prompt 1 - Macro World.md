# Map Prompt 1 - Macro World

```text
Create a large high-fantasy world map of Aereth in a dark mystical atlas style. The map should feel ancient, arcane, elegant, and legendary, with an ornate decorative border, subtle celestial motifs, glowing blue-teal magical lines, gold title lettering, and a rich parchment-meets-cosmic aesthetic. The world is not fully known, so large parts of the map must remain covered in fog, haze, or partially obscured landmasses to imply future continents and unexplored regions. Show West, North, East, Center, South. Preserve the Fractured Expanse in the center, greatly expanded; the Void Seas surrounding dangerous outer routes; and the Absolute Void in the far south. Show Valterra in the west/northwest, Lyssara in the west/southwest, Kaer Morr in the north, Nox Aetern in the northeast/east, Solmyr in the east/southeast, and Elderwyth in the southeast or east-south frontier. Add large fog-shrouded silhouettes of future continents in the far west, north, east, and central outer areas, but do not label them. Title the map “The World of Aereth”.
```
