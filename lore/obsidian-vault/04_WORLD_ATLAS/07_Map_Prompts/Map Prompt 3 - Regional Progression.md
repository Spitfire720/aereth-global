# Map Prompt 3 - Regional Progression

```text
Create a detailed regional progression map of Aereth, styled like a high-end fantasy MMORPG atlas. Emphasize progression from the western starter world into northern survival zones, then eastern advanced regions, then the central Fractured Expanse, and finally the southern void-endgame. In the West, show The Lantern Marches with nearby Hallowmere, Blackwake, Eldreach Quay, and routes connecting Valterra and Lyssara. In the North, emphasize Kaer Morr, grave-like landscapes, and memory-haunted frontier locations. In the East, emphasize Nox Aetern, Solmyr, Pyrexis, Red Basilica, Glassfall, and The Black Archive. In the Center, emphasize the Fractured Expanse. In the South, show the Void Seas descending toward the Absolute Void. Add a subtle legend for settled lands, unstable lands, disputed lands, fogbound lands, and voidbound lands.
```
