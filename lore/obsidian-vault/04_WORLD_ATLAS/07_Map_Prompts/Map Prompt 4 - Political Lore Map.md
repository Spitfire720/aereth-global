# Map Prompt 4 - Political Lore Map

```text
Create a richly annotated political and cultural atlas map of Aereth in ornate dark-fantasy style. Preserve the large Aereth geography with the six known continents, the Fractured Expanse, the Void Seas, the Absolute Void, and fog-hidden future lands. Highlight western regions influenced by record-keeping, frontier trade, and recovery culture; northern regions marked by clan survival and remembrance; eastern regions marked by doctrine, forbidden knowledge, archives, and fire-forged belief; central Fractured Expanse marked by instability and relic danger; southern regions marked by void contamination and endgame terror. Add subtle influence zones for Archivists of the Last Echo, The Cartotheca, Vestige Guild, Concord of Echoes, Synod of the Shattered Sun, and The Severed. Leave some places unlabeled, partially erased, crossed out, or redacted.
```
