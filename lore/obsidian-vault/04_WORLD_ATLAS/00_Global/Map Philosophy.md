# 🧭 Map Philosophy

Maps in Aereth are not neutral. They are arguments.

## Faction map styles
- **Cartotheca maps**: precise, grid-based, obsessed with accuracy.
- **Archivist maps**: redacted, annotated, historical, full of warnings.
- **Vestige Guild maps**: route-based, profit-focused, hiding valuable paths.
- **Concord of Echoes maps**: contradictory, layered, showing multiple versions.
- **Severed maps**: anti-maps, misleading, renamed, unstable.

A map can be useful without being fully true.
