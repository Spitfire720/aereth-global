# 🗺️ The First Known Map of Aereth

> [!map] Canon purpose
> The old/current map is not the complete world. It is the First Known Map: a western/central known-world atlas made after RE:FRAGMENT.

![[First_Known_Map_Reference.png]]

## What this map shows
- Known continents around the Fractured Expanse.
- The central wound of the world.
- Void Seas.
- The Absolute Void.
- Major known cities and regions.

## What this map does not show
- the full size of Aereth
- future fogbound continents
- hidden political regions
- all erased territories
- all true routes
- all faction-redacted lands

## Prototype issues
Duplicate labels, AI-garbled labels, small scale, no fog frontier system, and the world looks too complete. Treat it as a source artifact, not final cartography.
