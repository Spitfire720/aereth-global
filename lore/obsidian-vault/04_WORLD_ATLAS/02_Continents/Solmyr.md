# Solmyr — The Sunless Expanse

> [!map] Identity

## Summary
Solmyr was overexposed. Reality did not vanish there; it rewrote itself. The sun fractured, dimmed, and now burns without warmth. Its people believe RE:FRAGMENT was necessary.
