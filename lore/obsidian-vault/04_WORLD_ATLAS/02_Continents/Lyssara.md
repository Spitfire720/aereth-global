# Lyssara — The Shattered Coast

> [!map] Identity
> Profit, survival, adaptation

## Summary
Lyssara absorbed RE:FRAGMENT unevenly. Coastal cities vanished overnight, leaving jagged shorelines and seas that simply stop. Survivors learned instability could be exploited. Lyssara is transactional, not lawless.

## Cultural truths
- Nothing is permanent.
- Everything has a price.
- Survival favors the adaptable.
