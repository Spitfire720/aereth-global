# Kaer Morr — The Hollow Highlands

> [!map] Identity
> Endurance through loss

## Summary
Kaer Morr lost more land than it retained. Entire mountain ranges were erased, leaving cliffs into nothing. What remains is cold, quiet, and heavy with absence.

## Cultural truths
- Loss is inevitable.
- Strength is silence.
- Survival is remembering what no longer exists.
