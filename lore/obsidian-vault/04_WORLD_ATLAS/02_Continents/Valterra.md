# Valterra — The Anchored Heart

> [!map] Identity
> Stability through legacy

## Summary
Valterra was the core continent before RE:FRAGMENT, not politically, but structurally. Its old civilizations built with symmetry, repetition, anchors, and memory-binding architecture. When RE:FRAGMENT came, Valterra held.

## Cultural truths
- Traditions are sacred.
- Records are obsessively maintained.
- Buildings are repaired, never replaced.
