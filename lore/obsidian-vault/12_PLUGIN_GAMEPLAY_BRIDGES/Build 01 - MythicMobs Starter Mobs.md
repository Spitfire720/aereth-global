# Build 01 - MythicMobs Starter Mobs

## Canon purpose

The first Lantern Marches mobs are not random monsters. They are symptoms of roads, memory, reflection, ash, and early Fragment disturbance.

## Boundary

MythicMobs controls:
- enemy spawning
- enemy behavior
- hostile/ambient pressure
- encounter pacing

MythicMobs does not control:
- Remnant identity
- race selection
- Fragments
- Intent
- Disciplines
- permanent player state

## Build 01 mobs

- Roadstray
- Bent Road Drifter
- Mirrorfen Wisp
- Hollowglass Shardling
- Ash Mile Cinderling

## Design rule

Combat in the starter area should communicate Aereth’s tone before it tests player mastery.

A player should feel:
- the road is unsafe
- memory is unreliable
- anomalies matter
- retreat is possible
- the world is wounded, not merely hostile
