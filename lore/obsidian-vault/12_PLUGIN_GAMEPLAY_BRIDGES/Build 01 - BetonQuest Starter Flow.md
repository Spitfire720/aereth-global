# Build 01 - BetonQuest Starter Flow

## Status

Prepared as draft-ready starter quest configuration for Lantern Marches Build 01.

## Scope

BetonQuest controls:

- NPC dialogue
- Journal entries
- Starter objective flow
- Quest flags/tags
- Player-facing instructions

BetonQuest does not control:

- Race identity
- Fragment state
- Intent state
- Discipline identity
- Permanent RPG progression

## Questline

`After RE:FRAGMENT`

## Primary locations

- Lantern's Rest
- The Quiet Ledger
- The Bent Road
- Hollowglass Pool

## Primary NPCs

- Registrar Elian Voss
- Archivist Maera Vale
- Road Warden Tollen

## Canon notes

The quest introduces RE:FRAGMENT, Remnant identity, and world instability without fully explaining the world's metaphysics.

Fragments remain responsibility/perception/consequence systems, not quest loot or raw power rewards.
