## Lore
Survivors warped by ash, heat, salt, injury, and pressure. They adapted too well.

## World interpretation
The world reads Vireborn as bodies that learned to survive hostile continuity.

## Cultural note
Vireborn cultures value control, scars, and the discipline of not becoming only hunger.
