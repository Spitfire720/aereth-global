# 👤 Remnant

> [!locked] Core definition
> Remnant is the existential condition shared by all player characters.

A Remnant is a being that persists despite not being fully secure in reality after RE:FRAGMENT. The player is not automatically chosen. The player becomes important because Aereth begins remembering them.

## System relationship
- Fragment Level measures how stable and recognized their existence is.
- Awakening marks threshold changes in how the world reacts.
- Core Memory defines how a player remembers themselves.
- Titles define what Aereth remembers them as.

All players are Remnants. Not all players are [[Continuants]].
