# What is Aereth

Aereth is a Minecraft MMORPG world after RE:FRAGMENT, a world-changing continuity event. Maps lie, records matter, Fragments attach, Intent has consequences, and players become important because Aereth begins remembering them.

## Style rule
Player-facing, clear, atmospheric. No backend plugin jargon unless the page is specifically about commands.
