# 🌍 Aereth

> [!canon] Core definition
> Aereth is the world, server, and setting.

Aereth was once whole. Not peaceful. Not perfect. But continuous.

After [[RE-FRAGMENT]], Aereth became a damaged continuation of itself. Parts of the world remain. Parts are missing. Parts returned incorrectly.

## Core themes
- memory
- proof
- identity
- continuity
- survival
- contradiction
- preservation
- distortion
- what returned wrong

## World rule
Every place, creature, faction, item, quest, and system should answer:

> What did this thing remember, lose, preserve, or become after RE:FRAGMENT?
