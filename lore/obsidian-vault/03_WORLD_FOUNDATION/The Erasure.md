# 🕳️ The Erasure

The Erasure is the scholarly/system term for the continuity-damaging phenomenon associated with [[RE-FRAGMENT]].

It removed or destabilized reality, history, identity, memory, proof, and causality.

A fully erased person may leave no name, record, body, or memory.

## Known effects
- Maps become unreliable.
- Cities can remain without histories.
- Roads lead to places that should not exist.
- Some beings remember impossible lives.
- Some entities have no origin at all.

> [!warning]
> Use mystery, not confusion. Contradictions must feel intentional.
