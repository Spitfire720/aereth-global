# 🧩 RE:FRAGMENT

> [!locked] Definition
> RE:FRAGMENT is the world-changing event/condition that took place in Aereth.

Correct phrasing:

> You are playing in Aereth after RE:FRAGMENT took place.

## What happened
Reality did not simply break. It lost continuity.

Maps became unreliable. Cities remained without histories. Roads led to places that should not exist. Some beings remembered impossible lives. Some entities had no origin at all.

- The Unmaking — historical/old-world term
