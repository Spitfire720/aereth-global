# ✦ Gods and Godlike Things

> [!canon] Lock
> Aereth has no confirmed true gods.

“God” is a cultural label. Depending on whom you ask, a god could mean a worshipped force, ancient construct, surviving belief-remnant, powerful entity, corrupted memory, regional spirit, false divinity, or something from the Fractured Expanse.

Archivists may deny gods entirely. Tribal, folk, or religious cultures may worship local gods, old powers, sky-remnants, ancestors, beasts, or void signs.

Future content can introduce godlike events without confirming true gods.
