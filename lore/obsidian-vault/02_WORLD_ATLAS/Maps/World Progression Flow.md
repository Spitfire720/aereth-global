# World Progression Flow

## Core Flow

Aereth progression generally moves:

**Center-West starter → wider West → North → East → Center → South**

This is a broad journey logic, not a forced rail.

## Why West First

The West is the best place to begin because it still contains:

- stable settlements
- surviving routes
- registries and archives
- trade corridors
- early recovery cultures
- manageable Fragment disturbances

The player should start here because the world still has enough continuity to teach them what was lost.

## Why North Next

The North should ask whether the player can endure what cannot be fixed.

It introduces:

- survival pressure
- loss-memory culture
- clans and old obligations
- harsh travel
- frozen ruins
- grave-like landscapes

## Why East After North

The East is not just “harder”. It is more dangerous because it claims certainty.

It introduces:

- doctrine
- forbidden knowledge
- archive politics
- fire-forged belief
- mutation theories
- higher-consequence decisions

## Why Center Late

The Center is not a normal region. The Fractured Expanse is a wound in world logic.

It should involve:

- reality instability
- high Fragment pressure
- relic risk
- impossible terrain
- distorted routes
- late-game encounters

## Why South Last

The South is not normal expansion space. It represents endgame/finality.

It should involve:

- Void Seas descent
- voidbound contamination
- Absolute Void boundary
- major final arcs
- irreversible consequence

## Player-Facing Summary

Begin where roads still remember their names.

Survive where memory becomes law.

Cross where certainty burns.

Face the scar at the heart of the world.

Descend only when there is nothing left to lose.
