## Canon Usage Rule

These maps are **not exact satellite images**. They are in-world atlas interpretations, useful for player-facing geography, visual direction, website/world pages, and lore structure.

Exact biome boundaries, settlement placement, quest areas, dungeon coordinates, and level scaling must be defined separately in region design documents.
