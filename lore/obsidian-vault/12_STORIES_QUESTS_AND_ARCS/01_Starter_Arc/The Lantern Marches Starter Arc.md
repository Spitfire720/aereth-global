# 🕯️ The Lantern Marches Starter Arc

## Purpose
Teach Aereth through place, not exposition.

## Player learns
- Aereth is after RE:FRAGMENT.
- Roads and maps cannot always be trusted.
- Records matter.
- Fragments are not loot.
- Intent changes outcomes.
- Factions disagree about what should be preserved.

## Required beats
1. Player arrives at [[Lantern's Rest]].
2. Player encounters [[The Quiet Ledger]].
3. Player follows [[The Bent Road]].
4. Player sees a memory/reflection anomaly at [[Hollowglass Pool]] or [[Mirrorfen]].
5. Player encounters first Fragment disturbance.
6. Player sees faction disagreement over recording, hiding, exploiting, or releasing the event.
7. Player gets first route into wider West.
