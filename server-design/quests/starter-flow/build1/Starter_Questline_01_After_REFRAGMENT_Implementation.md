# Starter Questline 01 - After RE:FRAGMENT

## Purpose

This is the first playable questline for Aereth. It should teach the player what kind of world they are in without burying them under lore like a university lecture with torches.

## Player-facing fantasy

The player arrives in Lantern's Rest after RE:FRAGMENT has already happened. The settlement survives by keeping records, guarding roads, and refusing to trust memory alone.

## Required beats

1. Player meets Registrar Elian Voss.
2. Player is registered as a Remnant.
3. Player is sent to The Quiet Ledger.
4. Archivist Maera Vale explains RE:FRAGMENT in careful terms.
5. Player is assigned to follow The Bent Road.
6. Road Warden Tollen explains the route.
7. Player reaches Hollowglass Pool.
8. Player returns to Lantern's Rest.
9. Completion flag is granted.

## Canon guardrails

- Do not say the player receives a Fragment as loot.
- Do not say Fragments grant raw power.
- Do not confirm true gods.
- Do not explain RE:FRAGMENT fully.
- Do not make Lantern's Rest feel safe beyond its walls.

## Tone

Grounded, practical, a little haunted. NPCs should sound like people who survived by being useful, not exposition machines with legs.

## Completion result

The player should understand:

```text
Aereth is broken.
They are a Remnant.
Records matter.
Roads are unreliable.
The world reacts strangely.
Lantern's Rest is the start, not the answer.
```
