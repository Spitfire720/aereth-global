# Mob Balance Targets - Build 01

## Target player state

Assumption:
- Level 1-5
- starter/basic gear
- no advanced Discipline
- no real Fragment advantage
- possibly new to server mechanics

## Combat targets

| Mob | Time to kill | Danger | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Roadstray | 5-10 seconds | low | safe first enemy |
| Bent Road Drifter | 8-14 seconds | low | slow and atmospheric |
| Mirrorfen Wisp | 4-8 seconds | low-medium | movement annoyance |
| Hollowglass Shardling | 6-12 seconds | medium | anomaly warning |
| Ash Mile Cinderling | 6-12 seconds | medium | optional edge threat |

## Death rules

A new player should not die unless:
- they pull multiple mobs
- they ignore warning geography
- they enter anomaly edge zones too early
- they are AFK

## Tuning knobs

Reduce difficulty by:
- lowering Health
- lowering Damage
- increasing Cooldown on spawners
- lowering MaxMobs
- increasing LeashRange control
- moving spawners away from roads

Increase difficulty by:
- adding MaxMobs
- lowering Cooldown
- increasing Damage slightly
- adding mild debuffs
- adding environmental pressure later
