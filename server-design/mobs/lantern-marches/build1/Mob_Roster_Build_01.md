# Lantern Marches Mob Roster - Build 01

## Design principle

Starter mobs should teach the world tone, not punish the player. The first enemy layer should feel strange, hostile, and readable.

## Mob roster

### Roadstray

Basic hostile Remnant-adjacent roadside creature.

Role:
- starter melee enemy
- teaches basic combat
- appears on The Bent Road

Tone:
- something that used to be a traveler
- not undead in generic zombie terms
- memory-loss road horror

### Bent Road Drifter

Slow ambient pressure mob.

Role:
- makes road edges feel unsafe
- low damage, low speed
- encourages staying on the route

Tone:
- a body following a route that no longer exists

### Mirrorfen Wisp

Light magical nuisance.

Role:
- evasive movement
- very low health
- introduces anomaly behavior

Tone:
- reflection without a source
- attractive danger

### Hollowglass Shardling

Anomaly creature around Hollowglass Pool.

Role:
- first slightly dangerous anomaly mob
- reinforces that Fragment disturbances are not normal loot caves

Tone:
- broken reflection made hostile

### Ash Mile Cinderling

Optional edge threat.

Role:
- used near Ash Mile only
- slightly higher pressure
- not core starter road mob

Tone:
- residue of burned routes and failed crossings
