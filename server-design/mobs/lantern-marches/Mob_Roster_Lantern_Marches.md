# Mob Roster - Lantern Marches

## Design rule

Starter mobs should hint at RE:FRAGMENT without making the first zone feel like endgame apocalypse. These are low-risk distortions, not cosmic bosses wearing chicken hats.

## Starter mobs

| Mob | Role | Location | Difficulty |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Roadstray | Basic melee starter enemy | The Bent Road | Very low |
| Wick-Touched | Abandoned outpost enemy | Old Wick Post | Low |
| Mirrorfen Wisp | Ranged/light anomaly enemy | Mirrorfen | Low |
| Ashling | Environmental ash creature | Ash Mile | Low-mid |
| Hollowglass Shardling | Scripted anomaly wave enemy | Hollowglass Pool | Low-mid |
| Ledgerless | Rare named mini-mob | Quiet Ledger / Old Wick Post | Mid starter |

## Roadstray

A small distorted creature drawn to repeated road markers.

### Mechanics

- Slow melee.
- Occasional short dash.
- Low health.

### Drops

- Road Splinter.
- Worn Thread.
- Tiny chance: Bent Marker Chip.

## Wick-Touched

Former outpost dead/echoes distorted by old lantern failure.

### Mechanics

- Melee with weak burn or blind effect.
- Spawns near dead lanterns.

## Mirrorfen Wisp

A reflection-light entity.

### Mechanics

- Floats over water.
- Weak ranged attack.
- Brief invisibility or glow flicker.

## Ashling

Small ash-form creature from The Ash Mile.

### Mechanics

- Leaves short ash particles.
- Minor fire pressure, not lethal if player is paying attention.

## Hollowglass Shardling

Controlled anomaly enemy used in first Fragment disturbance event.

### Mechanics

- Spawns in short scripted wave.
- Low health.
- Blue-teal particle trails.

## Ledgerless

A rare named enemy, not required for first implementation.

### Lore

Something the ledgers failed to hold.

### Mechanics

- Slightly stronger enemy.
- Uses memory/vanish effect.
- Good candidate for first mini-boss later.
