# Build 01 Mob Roster - The Lantern Marches

## Roadstray

Type: starter hostile  
Location: The Bent Road  
World-state category: disputed lands  
Threat: low  

Lore:

A person, animal, or memory-warped shape that lost its route and never properly returned. Roadstrays gather near broken roads and old markers.

Gameplay role:

- first readable combat enemy
- teaches basic danger outside hub
- low movement pressure
- low damage
- small group limit

Do not:

- make it grotesque too early
- make it fast
- give valuable drops
- tie it directly to a Fragment reward

## Hollowglass Wisp

Type: anomaly hostile / magical starter enemy  
Location: Hollowglass Pool  
World-state category: unstable lands  
Threat: low to moderate  

Lore:

A reflective disturbance around Hollowglass Pool. It behaves like light remembering the wrong owner.

Gameplay role:

- first magical/weird enemy
- teaches anomaly zones
- introduces RE:FRAGMENT consequences visually

Do not:

- make it a boss
- overuse teleportation
- hard-counter new players

## Ledger Mote

Type: non-hostile ambience / optional future entity  
Location: The Quiet Ledger or anomaly edges  
World-state category: settled/unstable overlap  
Threat: none  

Lore:

Small motes of remembered script that gather near records touched by RE:FRAGMENT.

Gameplay role:

- ambient entity
- visual worldbuilding
- possible future quest guide
