# Lantern Marches Encounter Zones - Build 01

## Zone 1 - The Bent Road

Purpose:
- first unsafe route outside Lantern's Rest

Mobs:
- Roadstray
- Bent Road Drifter

Rules:
- spawns should not start immediately outside the hub gate
- road center should be safer than road edges
- no more than 2-3 enemies visible at once in early route

## Zone 2 - Mirrorfen edge

Purpose:
- first strange visual anomaly area

Mobs:
- Mirrorfen Wisp

Rules:
- wisps should feel evasive, not lethal
- keep player escape routes clear
- do not place spawners inside water unless intentionally tested

## Zone 3 - Hollowglass Pool

Purpose:
- first direct Fragment/anomaly disturbance area

Mobs:
- Hollowglass Shardling

Rules:
- higher warning tone
- more particles/ambience
- tighter spawn count
- stronger WorldGuard flags may apply

## Zone 4 - Ash Mile edge

Purpose:
- optional danger signal for future route

Mobs:
- Ash Mile Cinderling

Rules:
- not on main starter path
- should signal “not everything is for level 1”
