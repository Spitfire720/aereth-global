# Intent Tutorial Bridge

## Purpose

Introduce Intent as meaning/direction imposed by the player, not as a starting class.

## Build 01 state

```yaml
first_intent_prompt: clarity
```

## Why Clarity first

Clarity works as a safe tutorial-facing Intent because it frames perception, recognition, and understanding. It does not force the player into a combat identity.

## Tutorial language

Use this tone:

> Intent is not what you are. It is the direction your will leaves behind when the world pushes back.

Less tutorial-menu nonsense, more Aereth. Good. Finally.
