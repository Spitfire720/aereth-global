# Fragment Tutorial Bridge

## Purpose

Hollowglass Pool is the first low-risk Fragment disturbance. It teaches that Fragments are not loot and do not grant raw power directly.

## Canon rule

Fragments alter perception, responsibility, access, consequence, pressure, and world interaction. They do not simply add damage.

## Build 01 state

```yaml
first_fragment_contact: hollowglass_pool
```

## Trigger candidate

- Location objective near Hollowglass Pool.
- Click/interact with Oraxen Hollowglass Shard prop.
- BetonQuest dialogue with Archivist Maera Vale after returning.

## Do not

- Give a Fragment as an item.
- Reward direct stat power.
- Store Fragment identity in MMOItems.
