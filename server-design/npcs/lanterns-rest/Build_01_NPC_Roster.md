# Build 01 NPC Roster - Lantern's Rest

## Archivist Maerin Vale

Role: first registry NPC  
Location: The Quiet Ledger  
Function: starts registration and starter questline  

Tone:

- careful
- tired but kind
- believes records are protection
- avoids claiming certainty about gods or RE:FRAGMENT

Dialogue purpose:

- establish player as Remnant
- establish Aereth after RE:FRAGMENT
- explain that records matter
- send player to inspect a safe disturbance along The Bent Road

Sample line:

> "Name first. Certainty later. Around here, the ledger remembers what people cannot."

## Road Warden Tovan

Role: route safety NPC  
Location: Lantern's Rest road gate  
Function: warns player before leaving hub  

Tone:

- practical
- blunt
- protective without being soft

Sample line:

> "The road bends where it should not. Stay near the markers, and if the glass starts reflecting things behind your eyes, walk back."

## Vessa Quaymark

Role: trade/frontier NPC  
Location: trader corner  
Function: future shop/economy tutorial  

Tone:

- lively
- trade-minded
- Lyssaran influence

Sample line:

> "If the road stays open, goods move. If goods move, people stop panicking. Simple enough, until the road disagrees."

## Brother Halven

Role: local faith/folk memory NPC  
Location: small shrine/outpost ruin  
Function: soft introduction to cultural disagreement about gods  

Tone:

- humble
- not dogmatic
- believes names matter

Sample line:

> "Gods? Some say no such thing survived. Some say they were never there. I light the lantern anyway."
