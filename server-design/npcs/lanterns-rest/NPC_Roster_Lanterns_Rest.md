## Writing guidance

NPCs in Lantern's Rest should feel grounded. Their lives are practical: roads, records, trade, food, lamps, missing names, weird reflections.

Avoid making everyone cryptic. One cryptic NPC is atmosphere. Ten cryptic NPCs is just bad customer service in a cloak.
