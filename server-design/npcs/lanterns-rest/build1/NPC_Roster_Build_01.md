# Lantern's Rest NPC Roster - Build 01

## Registrar Elian Voss

Role: settlement registry, first player contact.

Personality: tired, precise, dry, cares more than he admits.

Gameplay role:

- Starts the first questline.
- Registers the player as a Remnant in dialogue flow.
- Sends player to The Quiet Ledger.

Location: Lantern's Rest registry desk.

## Archivist Maera Vale

Role: Archivist of the Last Echo outpost representative.

Personality: calm, unsettling, careful with words.

Gameplay role:

- Explains RE:FRAGMENT names.
- Explains Remnant as condition, not race.
- Sends player toward The Bent Road.

Location: The Quiet Ledger.

## Road Warden Tollen

Role: practical road guard / frontier route keeper.

Personality: blunt, superstitious, competent.

Gameplay role:

- Explains The Bent Road.
- Warns about Hollowglass Pool.
- Reinforces that routes are unstable.

Location: gate or road marker leaving Lantern's Rest.
