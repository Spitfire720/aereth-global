# Prop Visual Specs - Lantern Marches Build 01

## Registry Ledger

- Object: heavy open book / ledger on a small stand.
- Materials: dark wood, worn brass, teal-glowing page lines.
- Mood: official but decayed.
- Reads as: "you are being recorded."

## Bent Road Marker

- Object: crooked marker post, small hanging plate, route notch.
- Materials: old timber, iron nails, faded cloth strip.
- Mood: road survived, but direction is unreliable.

## Hollowglass Shard

- Object: translucent shard cluster emerging from ground.
- Materials: teal-blue glass, violet internal cracks.
- Mood: memory anomaly, not ordinary crystal.
- Must not look like generic amethyst.

## Lantern Waymarker

- Object: waist-high road lantern / shrine post.
- Materials: dark metal, warm lantern, teal rune glow.
- Mood: safe route, fragile civilization.

## Archive Crate

- Object: reinforced crate with wax seals, archive marks, rope.
- Materials: aged wood, pale labels, brass hinges.
- Mood: records moved during crisis.

## Quiet Ledger Stamp

- Object: small stamped paper/seal icon.
- Materials: ivory paper, teal ink, faded registry mark.
- Use: inventory/quest prop.

## Ash Mile Charcoal Post

- Object: burnt post, charred sign, ember cracks.
- Materials: blackened wood, dull orange glow.
- Mood: old fire damage, route warning.

## Mirrorfen Reed Charm

- Object: bound reeds, thread, tiny reflective charm.
- Materials: reeds, string, water-polished shard.
- Mood: folk protection against reflection-memory weirdness.
