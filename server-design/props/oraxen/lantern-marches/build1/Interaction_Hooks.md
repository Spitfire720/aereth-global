# Oraxen Interaction Hooks - Build 01

## Rule

Oraxen can present the object. It should not own story state.

## Registry Ledger

Possible future interaction:

- Click opens/continues BetonQuest dialogue with Registrar Elian Voss.
- FragmentEngine remains owner of Remnant/race/identity state.

## Hollowglass Shard

Possible future interaction:

- Click checks whether player has completed starter objective.
- FragmentEngine records anomaly exposure if needed.
- BetonQuest handles journal/task update.

## Lantern Waymarker

Possible future interaction:

- Click shows route hint.
- Could later integrate with waystone/fast travel system.
- No fast travel in Build 01.

## Archive Crate

Possible future interaction:

- Could be storage, decorative, or quest container.
- If storage is enabled, check WorldGuard and player permissions carefully.

## Build 01 approach

For now, keep interactions minimal.
Use these as placed visuals first.
Then add click actions only after quests and protections pass.
