# Build 01 Playable Slice - The Lantern Marches

## Purpose

Build 01 is the first playable slice of Aereth.

It is not the full Lantern Marches. It is not all early game content. It is the first proof that Aereth can feel like Aereth in-game.

## Core player fantasy

You are not beginning in a normal safe kingdom. You are beginning in a recovered frontier settlement trying to make sense of a world after RE:FRAGMENT.

## Build 01 area list

### Lantern's Rest

Role: starter hub  
World state: settled lands  
Risk: none inside protected areas  

Needed:

- spawn area
- registry/archive
- road gate
- trader corner
- route board
- visual line toward the Bent Road

### The Quiet Ledger

Role: registry and lore onboarding  
World state: settled lands  
Risk: none  

Needed:

- Archivist/Registrar NPC
- ledger prop
- map wall
- dialogue explaining Remnant condition carefully
- no lore dump

### The Bent Road

Role: first journey route  
World state: disputed edge / lightly unstable  
Risk: low  

Needed:

- broken road pieces
- old markers
- starter mob pockets
- subtle route weirdness
- visible fallback toward hub

### Hollowglass Pool

Role: first anomaly site  
World state: unstable lands  
Risk: low to moderate  

Needed:

- reflective water/glass effect
- weird lighting
- Hollowglass Wisp encounter
- investigation objective
- clue return item or quest flag

## Canon anchors

- The Lantern Marches sit between Valterra and Lyssara influence.
- The region is a starter subregion, not a full continent.
- The player learns about RE:FRAGMENT through local consequences, not a textbook speech.
- The hub represents recovery culture, record-keeping, and frontier survival.
