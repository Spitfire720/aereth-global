# Lantern Marches Subregions Build Plan

## Overview

The Lantern Marches should be built as connected route-based spaces rather than isolated biomes.

## Subregion table

| Subregion | Role | Risk | Main systems |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Lantern's Rest | Hub | Safe | NPCs, vendors, registry, first quests |
| The Bent Road | Route tutorial | Low | Navigation, weak enemies, road anomalies |
| Mirrorfen | Reflection anomaly | Low-mid | Puzzle, visual distortion, memory echo mobs |
| Old Wick Post | First combat pocket | Mid starter | Mob camp, abandoned outpost, recover records |
| The Ash Mile | Hazard route | Low-mid | Environmental pressure, ash mobs, route repair |
| Saint's Crossing | Cultural/story site | Low | Moral choice, local belief, faction hint |
| The Quiet Ledger | Archive annex | Safe/low | Lore, records, Fragment investigation |
| Hollowglass Pool | Fragment disturbance | Scripted | First controlled anomaly event |

## The Bent Road

### Visual design

- Road visibly bends in ways that make no practical sense.
- Mile markers repeat numbers.
- One broken bridge should show the route going nowhere, then continuing elsewhere.

### Gameplay

- Teach following route markers.
- Introduce weak distorted enemies.
- Introduce road repair or marker restoration.

### Mob ideas

- Roadstray.
- Bent Hare.
- Misremembered Hound.

## Mirrorfen

### Visual design

- Shallow water, fog, roots, half-submerged road stones.
- Reflections show ruined structures not present above water.
- Blue-teal lights under the water.

### Gameplay

- Light puzzle: find correct reflection marker.
- Dialogue with an echo NPC or memory remnant.
- Low-risk Fragment perception tutorial.

## Old Wick Post

### Visual design

- Collapsed watch post.
- Lanterns burned out except one blue flame.
- Old notices nailed to walls, text partially missing.

### Gameplay

- First meaningful enemy cluster.
- Recover registry satchel.
- Optional mini-boss later.

## The Ash Mile

### Visual design

- Long blackened section of road.
- Ash drifts, dead trees, charred signs.
- Occasional orange-red glow under cracked earth.

### Gameplay

- Teach environmental danger.
- Short escort or route-clearing quest.
- Low-level hazard without punishing new players too hard.

## Saint's Crossing

### Visual design

- Old road crossing with broken shrine/marker.
- No confirmed gods. Local people call it holy anyway, because people see a rock, feel dread, and immediately invent paperwork for the divine.

### Gameplay

- Introduce cultural ambiguity around gods.
- Player chooses whether to preserve, record, or ignore a local tradition.
- Small consequence flag, no permanent lock.

## The Quiet Ledger

### Visual design

- Small archive post.
- Ledgers chained to desks.
- Missing names visible as blank gaps.

### Gameplay

- Archivist quests.
- Turn in recovered records.
- Learn Fragments are not items.

## Hollowglass Pool

### Visual design

- Circular pool with glass-like water.
- Floating shards, reflected stars even during day.
- Controlled anomaly field.

### Gameplay

- First scripted Fragment disturbance.
- Small wave event.
- Unlock first world-recognition/title hint.
